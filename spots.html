Popular Spots

Blue Tokai – Hitech City

Most Ordered Item: Cold Brew Coffee

"

Blue Tokai is one of India’s most popular specialty coffee brands. Their Hyderabad branch offers freshly roasted beans sourced directly from Indian farms. The café has a modern yet cozy ambience, ideal for students and professionals who love working with coffee. They also conduct coffee brewing workshops and allow customers to buy freshly roasted beans for home use. If you’re looking for authentic third-wave coffee culture, this is the place to be.

Third Wave Coffee – Banjara Hills

Most Ordered Item: Caramel Latte

"

Third Wave Coffee has become a favorite for young professionals and college students. Known for its smooth caramel latte and handcrafted brews, the café also serves delicious croissants and cakes. The interiors are warm and minimalistic, making it perfect for quiet evenings or casual meetings. Their focus on sustainable coffee sourcing adds to the brand’s uniqueness. Whether you’re a coffee connoisseur or a casual drinker, you’ll find something here to love.

Over The Moon Cafe – Jubilee Hills

Most Ordered Item: Mocha

"

Over The Moon is one of Hyderabad’s trendiest rooftop cafes. With its terrace seating and panoramic views, it’s a hotspot for youngsters during evenings. Apart from coffee, they also serve continental dishes and snacks, making it a great hangout spot. The café is especially popular for its mocha, which combines rich chocolate flavor with strong coffee. If you’re looking for both good vibes and great drinks, OTM is a must-visit.

Italian Coffee Company – Gachibowli

Most Ordered Item: Espresso

"

Italian Coffee Company brings the authentic European café experience to Hyderabad. Their espresso is rich, bold, and strong—just the way traditional Italian coffee is meant to be. The café is compact yet lively, often filled with working professionals from nearby IT hubs. They also serve sandwiches, quick snacks, and pastries, making it perfect for a short coffee break. If you want to experience coffee the Italian way, this is your go-to spot.

Café Bahar – Secunderabad

Most Ordered Item: Filter Coffee

"

Café Bahar is an iconic name in Hyderabad, best known for its legendary biryani. However, their filter coffee also holds a special place among locals. The café has a simple, casual setup where families and friends gather for food and drinks. Many people enjoy a hot cup of filter coffee after a hearty biryani meal here. It’s not just a café but a part of Hyderabad’s food culture, combining tradition with taste.
